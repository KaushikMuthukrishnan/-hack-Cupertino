Know Your Nation

Welcome to the US!
Moving can be hard, especially when it is to a whole new country. You have to leave behind the places you know, the people you know, your friends, family, loved ones. But you made it here, to the United States of America. It is a diverse nation where people of all backgrounds are welcome and are able to coexist without judgement or exclusion. The idea of such a nation as this came about due to the people's inability to exercise their rights and experience true liberty and freedom. Thus the United States is a nation that is built upon the idea of inalienable rights to all individuals on its soil.
Site Overview
This site is built as a quick and handy resource to assist those who are unfamiliar with the ideals and foundations of the United States. As such, you will be able to find information on the inalienable rights and duties granted to every citizen of the US. Translations of this site are available under "More," as are printing options. We hope that these resources help in this vital information reaching large audiences, whether that be spreading the word of this site or handing out multilingual flyers of this site.
